Our Story

Legend Cinema was founded in 2011 with a vision to bring world-class cinematic experiences to Cambodia. Starting with our first location in Phnom Penh, we have grown to become the leading cinema chain in the country, known for our state-of-the-art technology, comfortable seating, and exceptional customer service.

We are proud to be a Khmer-operated company, dedicated to contributing to the local entertainment industry and providing a platform for both international blockbusters and local Cambodian films.

Our Mission & Values

Mission

To be the preferred entertainment destination by consistently delivering an unparalleled cinema experience through innovation, quality, and Khmer hospitality.

Our Core Values

  • Customer First: We prioritize the satisfaction and enjoyment of our patrons.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of our operations.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and ideas to enhance the movie-going experience.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Community: We are committed to supporting and engaging with our local communities.
